# ⚡ QUICK FIX - TIME PICKER AM/PM

## 🔴 Problem

User selected 12:00 AM (midnight) thinking it was 12:00 PM (noon)

00:00 (midnight) < 11:00 (morning) = Validation error ❌

---

## ✅ Solution

Changed time picker to **24-hour format**:
- **00:00** = Midnight
- **09:00** = 9 AM
- **12:00** = Noon
- **17:00** = 5 PM
- **23:00** = 11 PM

**No more AM/PM confusion!** ✅
